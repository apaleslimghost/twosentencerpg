Two-Sentence Role-Playing Games

The Premise

Make a role-playing game that fits into two sentences. Interpret "role-playing game" as loosely as you'd like. Include in those two sentences as much setting, game mechanics, character generation, and flavour as makes sense. Get creative with it. Keep it under fifty words; .

Here's an example:

The Board of Governors (i.e. the GM) resets the day if anything goes wrong in the Citadel, like the insurrection your ragtag bunch of misfits is planning. Roll a d6 every relevant piece of knowledge your character has retained between loops and compare the sum to the difficulty the GM sets.

Email twosentencerpg@ghost.computer with your submission, including the name you'd like to be credited with, and a URL for me to link to. Or, you could raise a Pull Request against the repository, if that's the sort of thing you can do.

This is just for fun, it's not a competition. I hope you'll find it inspiring.
